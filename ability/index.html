Server Rules

  • No Spam

    Please refrain from excessive and repetitive messages in the chat. Spamming can disrupt the experience for others.

  • No Advertisement

    We do not allow any form of advertising in the chat. This includes promoting other servers, products, or services unrelated to Ability SMP.

  • Cheating or Hacking

    Cheats and exploits are strictly prohibited. Please play fairly to ensure everyone has a good experience.

  • Keep Chat Friendly

    Keep the chat friendly and suitable for all ages. Inappropriate language, discussions, or content are not allowed.

  • No Lag Machines

    Lag machines are strictly prohibited. If found, this will result in a permanent ban. Any kind of large farm which can affect server performance should be reviewed by an admin before being put into use.

  • No Chunk Loaders

    All kinds of chunk loaders are prohibited because they can cause server lag.

  • Have Fun!

    Most importantly, have fun exploring and using your abilities!
